Hebrew font files are required for PDF generation.

Download Heebo font TTF files from Google Fonts:
https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Heebo

Required files (place in this directory):
- Heebo-Regular.ttf    (weight 400)
- Heebo-Medium.ttf     (weight 500)
- Heebo-Bold.ttf       (weight 700)

Steps:
1. Go to https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Heebo
2. Click "Download family"
3. Extract and copy the above .ttf files here

Without these files, Hebrew text in the generated PDF will not render correctly.
